Programs

We organized a special event to show our appreciation to the hardworking canteen workers. Through fun games and team-building activities, we made sure they felt valued and celebrated for their dedication. In addition to the games, we provided delicious meals to nourish them and show our gratitude. It was a heartwarming day that allowed us to give back to those who serve us so well.
